What is Minitea Snacks?

Minitea is a cat cafe founded by a group of girls who love bonding over tea, cats, and sweets. Because of the growing popularity of the cafe, we have decided to create another location to sell another thing we love the most: Snacks!

Our snacks are handcrafted and designed with tender loving care, based on the sweets we sell in the cafe itself, we've made the snacks so you can have a taste of Minitea at your own home! Better yet, we also sell four of our special blends so you can have your own Minitea party at the comfort of your home. We sincerely hope you enjoy our snacks!
